Why Most Real Estate Advertising Fails

Most agents hire generalist agencies that run the same playbook for dentists, plumbers, and realtors. The targeting is broad, the landing pages are generic, and nobody tracks whether leads actually close. Boosting posts is not advertising. Running Google Ads to your homepage is not a strategy.

Real estate advertising works when three things align: the right platform, creative that stops the scroll, and a landing page built to convert traffic into a lead you can call within minutes.

Three Platforms. One Goal: Exclusive Leads That Close.

Each platform reaches buyers and sellers differently. We build campaigns on the platforms that match your market, budget, and goals — then optimize relentlessly until the math works.

Google Ads

Capture buyers and sellers at the exact moment they are searching. Google Ads reach people with the highest intent — they are actively typing "homes for sale in Scottsdale" or "best realtor in Phoenix" into the search bar.

  • Google Search campaigns targeting high-intent real estate keywords
  • Google Local Services Ads for "near me" searches
  • Remarketing to website visitors who did not convert
  • Cost per lead typically $15 to $45 for buyer leads

Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)

Reach buyers and sellers before they start searching. Meta's targeting lets us find homeowners likely to sell based on life events, equity data, and behavioral signals — then serve them ads that feel personal, not spammy.

  • Buyer lead campaigns with listing-specific creative
  • Seller lead campaigns targeting homeowners with high equity
  • Vertical video Reels and Stories for top-of-funnel awareness
  • Lookalike audiences built from your past clients and closed deals

TikTok Ads

Reach younger buyers and first-time homebuyers where they spend the most time. TikTok's algorithm rewards creative that feels authentic — not polished corporate ads. We produce scroll-stopping content that generates leads at scale.

  • Home tour and walkthrough video ads
  • Market update and neighborhood spotlight content
  • Lead generation forms native to TikTok (no redirect needed)
  • Lowest cost per impression of any major platform

How We Build and Optimize Your Ad Campaigns

Every campaign follows a four-step process. No shortcuts. No templates borrowed from another industry. Built from scratch for your market.

Market Research & Strategy

We analyze your market, competitors, and target audience. We identify which platforms, keywords, and audiences will generate the highest-quality leads at the lowest cost for your specific ZIP codes.

Creative & Landing Pages

We build ad creative (images, video, copy) and custom landing pages designed to convert your specific audience. Every landing page is tested against benchmarks before a single dollar is spent.

Launch & Speed-to-Lead

Campaigns go live with speed-to-lead automation. Every new lead gets a text within 60 seconds, a notification to your phone, and a push to your CRM. You never miss a lead.

Optimize & Scale

We analyze performance data weekly. Underperforming ads are paused, winning creative is scaled, and landing pages are A/B tested. By month three, cost per lead drops 30 to 50 percent.
